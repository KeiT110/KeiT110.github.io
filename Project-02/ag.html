KALE AND KATSU SALAD

Ingredients Salad:

  • 4 cups chopped kale
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • ½ cup radishes, sliced
  • ½ cup green onions, chopped
Katsu:
  • 1 chicken breast (or tofu)
  • Salt and pepper
  • ½ cup flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying
Dressing:
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  • Katsu: Season chicken/tofu with salt and pepper. Dredge in flour, dip in egg, and coat with panko. Fry in oil until golden brown; slice.
  • Salad: Combine kale, carrots, tomatoes, avocado, cucumber, radishes, and green onions in a bowl.
  • Dressing: Whisk together soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, honey, and ginger.
  • Assemble: Toss salad with dressing, top with katsu, and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Serve immediately!
